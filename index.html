2008-2010: Where it began at NCBA

I began as a Relationship Officer at NCBA Group, supporting retail and SME growth. I evaluated loans, conducted site visits, and managed customer onboarding from end to end. This is where I built my core discipline in credit assessment, risk monitoring and client relationship management.

2010-2012: Premier relationship management at ABSA

As Premier Relationship Manager at ABSA Bank Kenya, I managed corporate and high-net-worth portfolios, drove cross-selling in trade finance, credit facilities and investment products, and delivered top-tier customer advisory with strong compliance discipline.

2012-2017: Global fintech execution at Ezra

At Ezra, I led global telecom-fintech relationships and delivery of airtime credit and micro-lending solutions. I coordinated product and commercial teams across regions, negotiated strategic contracts, and helped grow high-volume transactional platforms across diverse markets.

2017-2020: Regional partnership scale at Greenlight Planet

As Partnership Business Leader for East and South Africa, I managed key partner ecosystems, integrated PAYGO models, improved onboarding speed, and maintained strong retention and repayment performance in renewable energy distribution.

2020-2023: Strategic partnerships leadership across Africa

I managed a $20M+ partnership portfolio and helped scale Sun King through telecom-led distribution in 13+ African markets, including complex environments such as DRC and Madagascar, while collaborating with partners such as the World Bank and GIZ.

2023-2024: Commercial Director, Africa - 4Life Solutions

I led commercial scale-up of SAWA clean water technologies across Africa, built multi-sector pipelines, deepened government and development-partner relationships, and pioneered carbon-offset and CSR-linked financing models to improve affordability in underserved communities.

2024-Present: Founder at LynchPin Advisory

I have supported growth of a telecom-led fintech platform from 2 markets to 22+ markets and contributed to over $1M in quarterly revenue growth by optimizing PAYGO solar-fintech partnerships. I also advise global investors and startups on market-entry and cross-border scaling strategy.

The original banking story in my own words

I started in banking at Barclays (now ABSA), walking door-to-door with a folder of account, loan and credit card forms. It was relationship-selling before the digital age. Then I moved to Dubai, and after a year, Barclays (now NCBA) rehired me as a Premier Manager serving clients at Westlands Office, Park Branch and Westgate.

Dubai chapter: Al-Futtaim Group LLC
